About

Music Communication Researcher & Industry Professional

I am a music communication researcher and creative industry professional with over 10 years of experience spanning Taipei, Tokyo, Beijing, and Shanghai. 我是一名音樂傳播研究者與創意產業專業人士，擁有超過10年橫跨台北、東京、北京與上海的經驗。

My work bridges academic research and industry practice, exploring how digital platforms reshape music production, distribution, and consumption. I have authored 2,000+ editorial pieces for major music platforms and coordinated 100+ large-scale events including international festivals and television productions. 我的工作連結學術研究與產業實務，探索數位平台如何重塑音樂製作、發行與消費。曾撰寫2000餘篇音樂平台專欄，統籌100餘場大型活動包括國際音樂節與電視製作。

My research interests include platform economics, digital transformation in cultural industries, copyright governance, and the political economy of streaming media. 我的研究興趣包括平台經濟、文化產業數位轉型、版權治理與串流媒體政治經濟學。

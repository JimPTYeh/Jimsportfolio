About

Music Communication Researcher & Industry Professional

With over 10 years of professional experience in the creative industries across Taipei, Tokyo, Beijing, and Shanghai, I specialize in music communication, platform economics, and cultural industry studies. 擁有超過10年創意產業專業經驗，橫跨台北、東京、北京與上海，專精於音樂傳播、平台經濟與文化產業研究。

My work bridges academic research and industry practice, focusing on digital transformation, copyright governance, and the political economy of streaming platforms. 我的工作連結學術研究與產業實務，聚焦於數位轉型、版權治理與串流平台的政治經濟學。

Currently pursuing a PhD in Journalism and Communication at Fudan University, my dissertation examines the transformation of music publishing and distribution networks in Taiwan's popular music industry. 現為復旦大學新聞傳播學博士候選人，論文研究台灣流行音樂產業中音樂出版與發行網絡的轉型。

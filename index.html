Profile

Explore Jim

Music Communication Researcher & Industry Professional

I am a music communication scholar studying how digital platforms transform cultural production, with a particular focus on music industries. My research examines platform economies, media production networks, and the political economy of streaming media, with an emphasis on cross-regional dynamics in Greater China and East Asia. 是一名音樂傳播領域的研究者，關注數位平台如何重構文化生產，尤其聚焦於音樂產業的轉型。研究主要探討平台經濟、媒介生產網絡以及串流媒體的政治經濟，並關注大中華與東亞區域之間的跨區域互動。

Before entering academia, I spent over a decade working in the music and creative industries across Taipei, Tokyo, Beijing, and Shanghai. My professional experience spans artist management, digital content production, and large-scale live event coordination. This practitioner background informs my research on platform-based cultural production and the transformation of creative labor. 在進入學術界之前，於台北、東京、北京與上海累積了十餘年音樂與文化創意產業經驗，涵蓋藝人經紀、數位內容製作與大型活動統籌。這些實務經驗使我得以從產業內部視角出發，理解平台化文化生產與創意勞動的轉型。

My broader research interests include music communication, cultural industry studies, music art management, digital media platforms, and the impact of emerging technologies such as AI on creative production. 研究興趣亦涵蓋音樂傳播、文化產業研究、音樂藝術管理、數位媒體平台，以及人工智慧等新興技術對創意生產的影響。
